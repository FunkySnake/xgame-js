What's the story about

The rules

You are the Snake - a spaceship exploring the deep space. You are located near (but not too near) a giant black hole.

Your mission is to collect tiny asteroids rich on proteinium - the rarest material in the galaxy and beyond.

Beware of the fast rotating neutron star, that is captured by the black hole, in a vicious cycle. It's gravity is strong enough to splash you in pieces!

Let's Play!

  • Guide the Snake with your mouse
  • Collect all the asteroids in blue - green color
  • You can pause the game with 'p'
  • If you're hit by the giant ball it's game over. Don't worry, try again with 'n'
